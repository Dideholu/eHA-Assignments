My Perfect Trip Destination

My Perfect trip destination will be South Korea. For many travelers, South Korea a seamless fusion of old and new —a place where history lives beside cutting-edge innovation,
where bustling cities give way to tranquil islands, and where every meal feels like an experience of its own.

Jeju Island

I look forward to visiting the famous Jeju Island.
Indeed, Jeju is officially recognised by UNESCO as a World Natural Heritage Site, Global Geopark and Biosphere Reserve,
encompassing Korea's highest peak of Hallasan Mountain, the vast forested swathes of a semi-tropical national park,
a wild coastline and the world's longest underground lava tube.


Animals in Korea

I would really love to see some traditional animals exclusive to Korea.
On top of my lists is the Mandarin Duck.

Food

Korean cuisine is a rich tapestry of bold flavors, vibrant colors, and deep-rooted traditions.
Known for its emphasis on balance—spicy, sweet, salty, sour, and bitter flavors all play a role.
Korean food celebrates seasonal ingredients and harmony.
Signature dishes include bibimbap (rice with mixed vegetables and meat), bulgogi (marinated grilled beef), and samgyeopsal (grilled pork belly).
Korean street food is equally beloved, with favorites like tteokbokki (spicy rice cakes), hotteok (sweet pancakes), and kimbap (rice rolls) found in bustling markets.
