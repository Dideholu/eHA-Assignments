    Meals and Ingredients


    Meals Ingredients
    Ofada Rice
    and Sauce    		 Unpolished local rice, Water, Salt, Onions,
    Green ball peppers, Scotch bonnet peppers,
    Palm oil, Locust beans, Assorted meats, Seasoning

    Cooking Steps

    To prepare Ofada Rice:

    1. Rinse the Ofada rice thoroughly in cool water
    2. In a pot, add water and bring it to a boil.
      Pour the rice into the cooking water and boil for about 10 minutes.
    3. Drain the rice, rinse again and then
      cook it in hot water till it is soft and fluffy
    4. Add a pinch of salt to taste and set aside

    To Prepare Ofada Sauce:

    1. Blend the green ball peppers and scotch bonnets coarsely, leaving some texture.
    2. Heat palm oil in a pot over medium heat until it becomes smoky(bleaching),then allow it to cool slightly.
    3. Add the sliced onions and locust beans frying until the onions become soft.
    4. Add the blended peppers and fry until the oil starts to separate from the mixture, about 10-15 minutes.
    5. Add the assorted meats, and seasoning. Stir and let it simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
    6. Serve the Ofada rice with the sauce on the side.

    Meals and Ingredients

    Meals Ingredients
    Ewa
    Agonyin
    and Plantain    		 Brown Beans, Onions, Seasoning, Ripe plantain,
    Dried red chili peppers, Palm oil, Vegetable Oil

    Cooking Steps

    To Prepare Ewa Agonyin:

    1. Soak the beans in water for a few hours, then rinse thoroughlu.
    2. Boil the beans with chopped onions until very soft, almost mushy.
    3. add salt and seasoning to taste, then mash the beans slightly and set aside.

    To Prepare Agonyin Sauce:

    1. Blend the dried red peppers coarsely with little or no water.
    2. Heat the palm oil in a pan until it smokes slightly
    3. Add the chopped onions and fry until they become very dark brown
    4. Add the blended pepper and continue and continue frying until the oil separates from the pepper.
    5. Add salt to taste and let it simmer.

    To Fry Plantain:

    1. Peel and slice the plantains diagonally into thin pieces.
    2. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan and fry the plantains until golden brown.
    3. Drain on paper towels and seerve with the Ewa Agonyin.
